Pastor Adewuyi's welcome Speech

The love of God and His grace is what we need in a time like this. Jesus said "come unto Me all ye that labour and are heavy-laden..." Jesus is a burden-bearer. He actually came for that purpose.

What burden do i carry, you may want to say. The truth is as long as you were born of a woman, you bear a burden of sin upon yourself. Sadly, any soul that sin will die. This places everyone under the judgement of death, both now and eternaly. This is the work of Satan.
